# Product Guidelines

These guidelines define the visual, functional, and ethical standards for **Well-Check Family Shield**.

## 1. Design & UX Principles
- **Clarity Above All:** The interface must be legible and straightforward. Use high-contrast colors and large typography, especially for elder members.
- **Trust & Reliability:** The UI should convey a sense of calm and safety. Avoid alarmist visual language except for critical, life-threatening alerts.
- **Feedback Loops:** Provide clear confirmation for every user action. Use subtle haptic feedback for mobile interactions to reinforce certainty.
- **Accessible Interactions:** Support variable font sizes and high-contrast modes. Design for one-handed use where possible.

## 2. Technical Quality Standards
- **Real-Time Responsiveness:** Alerts and status updates must be delivered with minimal latency.
- **Offline Resilience:** The app must gracefully handle data drops, queuing critical health data for sync when connectivity is restored.
- **Battery Preservation:** Background monitoring must be optimized to ensure the app does not significantly impact daily device usage.

## 3. Privacy & Ethics
- **Data Sovereignty:** Users (Monitors and Protected Members) must have clear control over what data is shared and with whom.
- **Transparent Logging:** Provide a clear, immutable audit trail for all safety events and manual check-ins.
- **No Dark Patterns:** The app should never use deceptive UI to influence user behavior. All health tracking must be opt-in.

## 4. Communication Style
- **Empathetic Tone:** Use supportive, human-centric language. Avoid overly clinical or technical jargon.
- **Direct & Concise:** In emergency states, all communications must be brief and action-oriented.
